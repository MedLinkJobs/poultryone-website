OUR STORY

Built by the Industry. For the Industry.

PoultryOne was created to solve a problem every poultry farmer in India knows too well — critical business decisions made on WhatsApp messages, paper registers, and guesswork. We built the operating system the industry never had.

Our Mission

To give every poultry farmer, hatchery manager, feed miller, and live chicken supplier the same real-time visibility and operational control that large corporations take for granted — at a price that makes sense for their business.

Our Vision

A fully connected, data-driven poultry value chain across South and Southeast Asia — where every placement, every egg, and every kilogram of feed is tracked, costed, and optimised automatically.

Six applications. One platform. One login.
